Wear & Share

The first decentralized peer-to-peer platform for sharing your wardrobe among friends. Each stakeholder connected to the platform is helping create an ecosystem connecting young people with each other, fashion influencers, stylists clothing chains and other major retailers all around the globe.

Mission

EXTEND USE

An average wearable is only used about 7 times. By sharing we can increase use while decreasing the expenses spent on building your wardrobe.

INCREASE SATISFACTION

People are generally satisfied with about 20% of their wardrobes. Let those 80% satisfy your friends' and family fashion needs.

INSPIRE SHARING

Sharing wearables with friends and family has been done for centuries. We're just providing you with a modern twist to simplify it.

Inspire sharing to fulfil everybody's fashion needs

Wear & Share Ecosystem

Decentralized user interaction

Share

Take pictures of your wearables to store them in your virtual wardrobe. Add friends and family to combine and expand your wardrobe. Browse and borrow from a huge joint wardrobe using just one finger.

Organize

Add information and tags such as color, size, season and brand to your wearables to organize your wardrobe to your liking. Have everything where you want it. And find it with ease when you need it.

Follow

Follow friends on your in-app feed. See how your clothing completes their look. See and borrow new additions to their wardrobe. Let your friends know about the wearables you love by commenting and liking.

Discover

Let us combine outfits for you. Our machine learning algorithms will learn your likes and dislikes to create your perfect look. All you have to do to take everybody’s breath away is complete the outfit by borrowing missing wearables from friends.

Group up

Create a private group for sharing wearables share to specific friends only. Or use groups to create themed wardrobes for you and your friends. Attending an event with a specific dress code? Sounds like a new group is forming.

Stylists

Create amazing outfits

The Wear & Share ecosystem will feature a wide array of proffessional stylists who will be able to help users create special outfits when going to important events.

Influencers

Public wardrobes

Every user on our platform will have the ability to become a fashion influencer by making their wardrobes publicly visible and having others follow their trends.

Collaboration

Fashion influencers are given a new outreach to their target audience while also provided with new types of collaborations with our partners and stakeholders.

Brands and retail

Wear & Share Business

The Wear & Share Business account will provide brands and retail the option to add new wearables and outfits to their wardrobes which users will be able to find on our partners’ public profiles.

Recognition and purchases

Users following our partner brands and retail will start seeing our partners’ items and outfits on their feeds where a simple option to purchase and add them to their wardrobes will be presented.

Society & Environment

Reducing the impact

By extending the average wearable lifeteime by a third the carbon savings alone equate to approximately those of 1620 miles driven by a modern car.

Charity

When our algorithm discovers some piece has not been worn for a long time and it won’t be worn again, a donation is stimulated to help those with nothing to wear.

All this is powered by the blockchain-enabled Wear & Share Token

Token

The blockchain-enabled platform allows us to use and distribute our own utility tokens, WSTokens, used to power three distinct featuers of the Wear & Share ecosystem.

Professional Stylists

Have an important event coming up? Pay our professional stylists some WSTokens to pick out an outfit for you to make sure you'll make a great first impression.

Premium wearables

Borrow or buy premium wearables from our brand and retail partners using WSTokens.

Advertising space

Promote your wearables and outfits using WSTokens within the app by completing users’ outfit recommendations or presenting them in our users’ feeds.

Token Distribution

• 65% sold to the public to fund the enterprise.

• 12% offered to founding team members and early future hires.

• 6% for advising on early development and business decisions.

• 5% offered to users as the refferal, social and bug bounty rewards.

• 12% dedicated to take care of unexpected business costs.

Token Sale

All the tokens sold will be used for development, marketing, operational, legal and administrative expenses.

• 40% for the development of the platform and it's components.

• 35% for attracting and retaining a base of users and promoting the brand.

• 20% to satisfy all the operational needs of the enterprise.

• 5% for legal and administrative services accrued by the business.
